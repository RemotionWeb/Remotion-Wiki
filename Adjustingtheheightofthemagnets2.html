Adjusting the height of the magnets

The magnetic force that allows Proteus Safe to drive on vertical surfaces can vary depending on:

  • Surface material
  • Surface thickness
  • Cleanliness of the magnets
  • Distance between the magnets and the surface
    • To ensure that the maximum magnetic force is available to hold Proteus Safe onto a surface, it can be necessary to adjust the position of the magnets. The operation is described in the following table:

CAUTION: Place the robot on a non-magnetic surface such as wood or concreate while doing the operation. 


Main Magnet

#

Description

Prepare the following tools:

X2

Wrench Size 17

Wrench Size 13



Hex Key Size 6



To access the main magnet adjustment bolts remove the main wheels by undo the 8 Allen screws and washers on both wheels. 

Remove the 4 M8 hex bolts & Nuts on each side and the 2 M10 hex bolts & Nuts in the middle of the magnets

Adjust the hight of the magnets by sliding it up and down until the right position is reached and the holes are aligned. The to be set ground clearance is marked on the mounting plates by each hole. 
Available mounting plate sets: 
Set 8-12mm 
- 2 of 120535 and 1 of 120951 with adjustable clearance of 8; 10 or 12mm

Set 12-16mm
- 2 of 121615 and 1 of 121616 with adjustable clearance of 12; 14 or 16mm


Location of the mounting plates

When the desired position is reached, install the 4 M8 hex bolts & Nuts on each side and the 2 M10 hex bolts & Nuts in the middle of the magnets 

Remount the wheels on each side.


Secondary Magnets

#

Description

Prepare the following tools:


Hex Key Size 4 & 5







To access the rear magnet adjustment bolts remove the omni wheels on both sides by undo the countersunk hex screws and lock covers. 

Remove the 6 hex screws on each side holding the coaxial adjustment shaft.

Adjust the hight of the magnets by rotating the coaxial shaft. Align the screw holes at the required setting. Repeat on both sides! 

The clearance of the secondary magnets can be adjusted between 5 – 15mm.


 

CAUTION! The setting on both sides must be identical and symmetrical!

Reinstall and tighten the 6 Hex screws on both sides!

Remount the omni wheels on each side.
