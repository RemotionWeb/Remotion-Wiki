Mounting on linear tool.

Install the top mounting bracket on the top of the robot. Choose a position as required. Use 2 of M6x25 screws with the 2 of M8 washers.

Install the camera mast onto the bracket by 1 of M6x30 and 1 of M6x35 screws, M6 washers and nylon nuts .
