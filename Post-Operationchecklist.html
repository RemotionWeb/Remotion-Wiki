Post-Operation checklist

Before operating the Proteus Safe System, it is essential to perform a series of checks to ensure the system is safe, functional, and ready for operation. The following pre-operation checks must be completed by the operator

#

Description

Comment

Inspect the robot for any signs of wear, damage, or loose parts resulting from the operation.



Check all cables, hoses, and connectors for any signs of wear or damage.



Clean any foreign debris from the robot using compressed air, if applicable



Check the tool for any wear or damage.



Inspect all glass on the equipment, such as the camera and lights, for damage.



Verify that magnet is clean and free from debris



Check if all auxiliary equipment, such as the wireless controller, chargers, batteries are in its designated locations.



Document any issues encountered during operation in the maintenance log for corrective maintenance.
