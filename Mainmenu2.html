Main menu

The main window, as depicted in the image below, will be displayed when starting the software:



The top panel on the screen displays the status of the video stream, followed by the source of the stream loaded into the pipeline. 

Please ensure that the correct source is configured. Currently, we are working with various camera sources.

For example, the source format for the blue cameras is: rtsp://User:1234@192.168.1.10:554/stream1

The buttons displayed on the main screen:

RemCam Interface

Play

Start video streaming from the RTSP source link shown in the top panel.


Pause

Pause the video stream.


Stop

Stops the video stream.


Settings

Opens settings menu.


Send to background

If you are viewing multiple camera streams, you can press this button to switch between them.


You can drag and drop the screen to a new position by holding the top bar. 

A single click on the screen will hide the top and bottom panels. 

Double-clicking on the screen will toggle between full-size and small-size modes.
